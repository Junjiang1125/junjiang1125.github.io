Creative Brief

Brand: All Nippon Airways(Japan)
Objective:
Print & social media ad campaign to demonstrate the excellent service provided by ANA,Such as luggage NEVER LOST..
Why does our client need to advertise?
After the epidemic,the number of tourists has increased,and we need to promote the flight experience of our high-quality service, especially in details such as luggage..
What is advertising going to accomplish?
We hope to inspire customers to travel and pay attention to the perfect experience of us, let customers think about us at first.
Who are we going to connect with?
People are often travel.Educated,above middle income pursuing quality life and good service.
What are the most insightful things we know about them?
They concern about comfort, luggage, and quality of service. Don't want to be very frugal and cheap.
What is the single most effective message that we can convey to them?
We are an airline committed to high quality service, provid ing comfortable surroundings in every detail.
What else is there to know that supports this mes- sage?
Especially we never lost luggage for 71 years.
