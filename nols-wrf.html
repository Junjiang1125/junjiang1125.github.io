NOLS WRF
Advertising & CamPaign

Wilderness First Responder course is designed specifically to provide you with
the tools to make critical medical and evacuation decisions in remote locations.
This project is for a non-profit organization, we need to find people who love to
help others.

Creative Brief

1. Why are we advertising?
This project is for a non-profit organization,we need to find people who love to help others.To build communities for those people.
2. Who are we talking to?
People who enjoy the outdoors and like to socialize sports, and live outdoors.
3. What do they currently think?
"The WFR program is for outdoor medical professionals, not people like me."
4. What is the single most persuasive idea we can convey?
"More and more people are outside; I want to be ready to help if needed."
5. What would we like them to think after seeing our message?
WFR is not for medical professionals, but for people who care about and want to help others in the backcountry.
6. Why should they believe it?
Experienced training is available around the world.
7. Are there any creative guidelines?
Showing the consequences if people not being helped in outdoor, and the impact on the environment.
