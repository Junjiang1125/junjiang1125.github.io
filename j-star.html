Introduction

J Star

Location: Hangzhou, China. Contemporary Dining Bar. It is a high-end bar mainly for afternoon tea and dinner. Its decor and dishes are exquisite and inspir- ing. There are usually dances, concerts, fashion shows, etc. On weekends.

About the Environment

It is divided into two completely different parts, day and night.
Daytime green background and flowering plants on the garden. Enjoy exquisite tableware and food in the beautiful sound of the piano, turning it into a dreamy and elegant setting.
The night will be lively with melodious music, alcohol and chatter. Elegant decoration, bright colors, modern music, will give you a perfect experience.

About the Food

Based on molecular gastronomy recipe. Low temperature and slow cooking: It is the principle proposed by the two founders of Molecular Cooking. Cooking at 50-60 degrees will refine the taste of food. Liquid nitrogen: In fact, it is a technol- ogy that sprays raw materials with a liquid nitrogen gun to make it freeze in a very short time. Foaming technique: Foaming mousse is made thanks to the cream bottle.

Competitive Analysis

WAKE TOWN is more inclined to the literary style, using more wooden decorations, and paying more attention to the taste and details of the ingredients.
GAGA is better at brand planning, impressive logo and interior design. The ubiquitous influ- encer promotion on social media will make you want to try it.

Mission Statement

J star is located next to the lively music square and luxury shopping mall. It is designed to interpret an elegant culinary experience, incorporating the con- cept of all-day dining. In a space full of comfort and creativity, modernity and elegance are incorporat- ed, setting a new benchmark for the city.
We creates a modern aesthetic experience for the overall atmosphere. When night falls, our bar lounge transforms from an elegant coffee salon into an intimate space for after-dinner drinks and cocktails.
